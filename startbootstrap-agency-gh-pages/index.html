About Me

A Brief Story Of My Hobby...

  • 2007-2009

    My Humble Beginnings..

    For almost a 10 years ago I noticed that photographying is that hobby I love. So I started to improve my skills for the first time using my mobile phone, I shooted a lot of average "photos", then its quantity and quality were rising higher and higher. I had an amazing cameraphone Sony Ercsson Cyber-Shot C702i with fabulous 3.2mpx Carl Zeiss Lenses. It was a period I concluded I love photographying, so...withing a 6 years I have got a Nikon D5100 DSLR Camera with a Nikon AF-S Nikkor 18-105mm f3.5-5.6 G lense. From day to day I think my skills became better and You can help me to improve it, just follow me.

  • February 2012

    Solid photo idea is born

    Me & my wife have gota new Nikon Coolpix P500 DSLR Camera, fabulous 36x ultrazoom. Later on I've already been making a more better shots, incl panoramas and HDR-photos - one of my favourite photo genres!

  • March 2014

    Transition to DX Format

    Dream had become true - my dear wife have brought me a new DSLR Camera - Nikon D5100 - Journey Begins!!

  • April 2014

    Phase One Early Commercial Photo

    It was a first commercial prders - I have got a goal to develop photobook - my favourite part of photography. So, I started to shoot in kindergartens and schools for albums, it was first huge earnings!
